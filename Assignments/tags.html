Kajal Aggarwal Movies

Kajal Aggarwal is a popular Indian actress known for her work in Telugu, Tamil, and Hindi films. With her versatility and charm, she has won the hearts of millions. Over the years, she has delivered a variety of performances in films across different genres.

Popular Movies of Kajal Aggarwal

  • Magadheera (2009) - A historical fantasy action film, one of the biggest hits of her career.
  • Thuppakki (2012) - A blockbuster Tamil action thriller where she starred opposite Vijay.
  • Singham (2011) - The Bollywood action drama, starring alongside Ajay Devgn.
  • Businessman (2012) - A Telugu action film with Mahesh Babu.
  • Special 26 (2013) - A Hindi heist thriller showcasing her acting range.

  1. Expressive Eyes: Kajal is known for her expressive eyes, which convey a wide range of emotions.
